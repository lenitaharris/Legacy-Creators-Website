What happens if we’ve contacted you

If you received a call, letter, or email from Legacy Creators, it’s because our audit flagged funds that appear to be owed to you (or to your business, or to a family member/estate you’re connected to).

Step 1: We reach out We attempt to contact the rightful owner. We will never ask for payment to “check” if you’re owed funds.
Step 2: We verify ownership We confirm identity and entitlement so those funds can be released to the correct person — not someone pretending to be you.
Step 3: Funds get released After proper verification, funds are disbursed. You receive your money. We are compensated only after successful release.

That’s it. You are not paying us in advance, you’re not “buying” anything, and you’re not gambling on a possibility. We only move forward once we’ve confirmed that real funds are being held in your name.

Your privacy

  • Your information is handled confidentially.
  • We will never sell your information to marketing lists.
  • We contact you for one reason: funds appear to be owed to you.
