Who we are

Legacy Creators is a private audit firm. Our focus is simple: identify funds being held by government entities that rightfully belong to private citizens or businesses, verify ownership, and help ensure those funds are returned to the correct party.

Many people never know they’re owed anything. Money can sit in holding accounts for years — untouched — because notices were missed, addresses changed, or the rightful owner wasn’t aware funds existed. After a certain amount of time (dependent upon county), the funds rightfully owed to you "escheats" to the government and becomes unrecoverable. We exist to prevent that money from being lost, absorbed, or otherwise escheating.
